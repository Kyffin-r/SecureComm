How To Use SecureComm

1. Generate PGP Keys: Fill in your name, email, and a strong passphrase to generate your PGP keys.

2. Encrypt a Message: Paste the recipient's public key and your message, then click "Encrypt" to secure your message.

3. Decrypt a Message: Paste your private key, enter your passphrase, and the encrypted message to retrieve the original message.

Enjoy secure communication!

About SecureComm

SecureComm is a user-friendly toolkit designed to simplify the process of secure communication using PGP (Pretty Good Privacy) encryption. Our goal is to make encryption accessible to everyone, ensuring that your messages remain private and secure.

History of PGP

PGP was created by Phil Zimmermann in 1991 as a way to provide privacy and authentication for email communication. It uses a combination of symmetric-key cryptography and public-key cryptography to secure messages. Over the years, PGP has evolved and is now widely used for secure communication across various platforms.

PGP has played a significant role in the development of modern encryption standards and continues to be a vital tool for privacy advocates and security-conscious individuals.
